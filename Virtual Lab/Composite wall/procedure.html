Test Procedure for Determining Thermal Resistance and Conductivity of the Composite wall

AIM:To determine total Thermnal resistance and Thermal Conductivity across the width of composite wall.

APPARATUS:
The apparatus consists of central heater sandwcihed between two sheets, dimmer stat, digital voltmeter and ammeter.

SPECIFICATIONS:
Enclosure size : 300*300*300 mm
Slab diameter : 200 mm
Thickness of mild steel (b1) = 25 mm
Thickness of Bakelite (b2) = 20 mm
Thickness of wood (b3) = 18 mm
Total Thickness of slab (b) = 63 mm

PROCEDURE:
1. Arrange the plates in proper fahion on both sides of heayter plates.
2. Opearte the hand press to ensure perfect contact between plates.
3. Close the box by acrylic cover sheet to achieve proper environmental condition.
4. Start the main switch amd switch on the heater.
5. Adjust the voltage input to heater through dimmerstat.
6. After reaching the study state, take the readings of 8 thermocouples, voltmeter and ammeter.
7. Note down the readings in observation table.
8. Make the dimmer knob to "zero" position and then put main switch off.
